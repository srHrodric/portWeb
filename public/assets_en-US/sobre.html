About me

Hello! I'm Rodrigo Santos, a developer with more than two years of experience in the industry and in freelance work. My professional career started with PHP, and, over time, I focused on improving my skills in JavaScript and Node.js, fundamental technologies for modern web development.

During my career, I have had the opportunity to lead and contribute to several projects, including:

  • API development;
  • Integrations with ERP services;
  • Creation of Landing Pages;
  • Development and implementation of multiplatform web systems.

In many of these projects, I acted as the main developer, being responsible for the complete logic of the applications, from the Front-end to the Back-end, using JavaScript and Node.js efficiently. My experience ranges from solving challenging technical problems to designing scalable and well-structured solutions.

Currently, I am dedicated to the creation of projects aimed at use on the web, focusing on compatibility between different devices. I am responsible for both the visual interface and the logic of systems, developing robust Back-end structures and integrating them with APIs of various services. My goal is to deliver complete, functional and scalable solutions, aligned with customer needs and market demands.
